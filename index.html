About Str8 Flight Entertainment

Str8 Flight Entertainment is a Belize-based music and media platform founded by Denton Barrow along with his brothers Cleon Barrow and Fenton Barrow. The team is dedicated to promoting authentic music and Caribbean culture, providing artists with a professional space to share their work with a global audience.

The Barrow brothers are actively involved in music production, recording, and creative direction, ensuring that each project reflects originality, quality, and the real journey behind independent music creation.

Through Str8 Flight Entertainment, the Barrow brothers aim to elevate Belizean and Caribbean artists by combining creativity, discipline, and modern digital platforms. The brand’s vision includes expanding into artist features, educational content, and cultural insights that inspire the next generation of creatives.
