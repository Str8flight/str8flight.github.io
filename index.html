About

Str8 Flight Entertainment is a Belize-based music and media platform founded by Denton Barrow along with his brothers Cleon Barrow and Fenton Barrow. The team promotes authentic music and Caribbean culture while giving a platform to emerging artists.

They are actively involved in music production, recording, creative direction, and artist development.
