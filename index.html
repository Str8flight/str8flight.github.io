About

Str8 Flight Entertainment is a Belize-based music and media platform founded by Denton Barrow along with his brothers Cleon Barrow and Fenton Barrow.

The Barrow brothers are actively involved in music production, recording, creative direction, and artist development.

Str8 Flight Entertainment also engages with local communities through events, workshops, and collaborations.
