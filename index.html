About Str8 Flight

Our Story

Str8 Flight Entertainment is a Belize-based music and media platform founded by Denton Barrow and his brothers Cleon & Fenton Barrow. We promote authentic music, Caribbean culture, and support emerging artists.

Mission

Our mission is to provide a global platform for Belizean talent, engage local communities, and inspire creativity through events, workshops, and collaborations.
